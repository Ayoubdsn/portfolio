Process

From Idea To
Scroll-Stopping

Every thumbnail I design goes through a deliberate five-step process —
strategy first, aesthetics second.

01
Step 01

Discovery

I study your channel, your niche, and your top competitors. Understanding what's already working in the feed — and where the gaps are — defines every creative decision that follows.

Channel Audit Competitor Research Niche Mapping CTR Benchmarks
02
Step 02

Hook Extraction

From your video content, I isolate the single most powerful visual hook — the emotion, the tension, or the curiosity gap that will make viewers click. One clear message. Zero noise.

Visual Hook Emotion Mapping Curiosity Gaps Message Clarity
03
Step 03

Concept Creation

I sketch multiple directions — different compositions, color stories, and emotional tones. You see options, not just one guess. The best concept moves forward; the rest sharpen the brief.

Multiple Concepts Composition Color Theory Visual Hierarchy
04
Step 04

Design & Refinement

The chosen concept comes to life — layer by layer. Typography, color grading, expressions, contrast. Each revision tightens the visual logic until every pixel is doing its job.

Typography Color Grading Contrast Iterations
05
Step 05

Delivery

You receive production-ready files — high-res PNG, source file, and all the specs needed to publish. Clean, fast, zero friction. Ready to go live the moment it lands in your inbox.
